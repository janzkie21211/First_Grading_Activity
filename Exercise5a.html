Information Technology Specialization

In academia, specialization (or specialisation) may be a course of study or major at an academic institution or may refer to the field in which a specialist practices.
In the case of an educator, academic specialization pertains to the subject that he specializes in and teaches..

CYBERSECURITY, DIGITAL FORENSICS & SYSTEMS SECURITY
The cybersecurity specialization centers around the security of applications, data, and networks, as well as the proper management of information technology. There is much overlap with digital investigations, as one of the main functions of this specialization is a type of quality assurance. Students explore topics such as information security, ethical hacking, information assurance, steganography, disaster recovery, vulnerability analysis and control, business continuity response, coding security, securing databases, mobile device forensics, operating system security, legal and ethical issues, auditing, and intrusion detection. Utica College hosts a fully online BS in cybersecurity with a focus in cybercrime and fraud investigation thatâ€™s designed for prospective IT professionals.
DATA ANALYTICS
Data analytics students design, develop, and manage data resources to make traditional systems more fluid and responsive to client specifications. With internet-based data management considerably on the rise, IT professionals who specialize in data analytics management are adept in predictive modeling, utilizing big data sample data sets to solve real-world problems. Colorado Tech offers a fully online BSIT in data management that can give candidates the edge they need to succeed in this sector of information technology.
DIGITAL INVESTIGATIONS
The digital investigations specialization centers around skilled, educated information technology professionals who can counteract the growing threat of crime on the internet. There is much overlap with cybersecurity and network and systems security. Students complete courses in vulnerability analysis and control, information security, information assurance, ethical hacking, mobile device forensics, disaster recovery, steganography, business continuity response, securing databases, coding security, operating system security, legal and ethical issues, auditing, intrusion detection, and digital law. The American Intercontinental University offers a fully online BSIT with a specialization in digital investigations.
DIGITAL SYSTEMS TECHNOLOGY & EMBEDDED SYSTEMS
IT professionals produce digital applications as operational answers to sophisticated challenges in systems technology. Experts in the design and production of these applications, embedded systems, smart technologies, and intelligent devices are particularly well-prepared for a career in digital systems technology. The Illinois Institute of Technology offers a graduate program in digital systems technology and embedded systems. The curriculum immerses students in intelligent devices, mesh networking, the internet of things (IoT), distributed sensor networks, and embedded systems.
NFORMATION TECHNOLOGY ENTREPRENEURSHIP
For this specialization, managerial and entrepreneurial skills are needed, as well as an instinct for thinking ahead of the curve. Information technology business students learn how to launch and maintain a new enterprise in the tech industry. The Illinois Institute of Technology hosts both a BSIT and an MSIT in management, entrepreneurship, and innovation, both of which feature courses in data analytics, systems technologies, information technology theory, and much more.
INFORMATION SYSTEMS MANAGEMENT & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY MANAGEMENTI
This specialization is concerned with the design, testing, and building of information systems that management can use to make organizations more effective in their day-to-day activities. An entirely online BS in information systems from Grantham University educates students on what they need to know to be an IT or information systems manager
NETWORK ADMINISTRATION & MANAGEMENTI
This specialization focuses primarily on network applications and their management. It overlaps considerably with information systems management or information technology management. Walden University hosts an online BSIT program with a specialization in networking and operations.
SOFTWARE DEVELOPMENT, ENGINEERING & ANALYSISI
This specialization focuses on programming and the development of sophisticated applications for use in public and private entities. This area dovetails with the computer science field of coding. Specialists use programming languages to build applications and programs that address the specific needs of the client. Durham Tech offers an associate degree in IT with a specialization in software development.
SYSTEMS ADMINISTRATION & ANALYTICSI
This specialization is concerned with the administration and management of servers used for the deployment of customer or client solutions deliverables. Bellevue University hosts the systems and network administration program (SNAP).
WEB & APPLICATION DEVELOPMENTI
This specialization focuses on programming and the development of sophisticated websites and applications. This area overlaps with the computer science field of coding or programming. Web and application development specialists use programming languages to build software program solutions for certain identified IT problems. The New Jersey Institute of Technology hosts both a BSIT and MS in IT web development.
